OUR STORY

More Than Just a Travel Company.

About EpicBliss Travels

At EpicBliss Travels, we believe travel is about so much more than just visiting new places. It's about creating unforgettable experiences, building friendships that last well beyond the trip, and discovering a version of yourself that only comes out when you step outside your comfort zone. Every journey we plan is built around that simple idea — that the best adventures change you a little, and they're even better when shared with the right people.

Where It All Began

EpicBliss Travels was born out of a genuine love for exploring the extraordinary. What started as a passion for discovering new places grew into something bigger — a desire to share that same sense of wonder with others. Today, we curate thoughtfully planned group journeys that bring together adventure, nature, culture, and comfort, so that every trip feels both exciting and effortless. Whether it's chasing sunrises over the Himalayas, walking through centuries-old trails, uncovering hidden gems most travellers never find, or simply swapping stories around a campfire under the stars, every itinerary we design is meant to leave you with memories that last a lifetime.

Who We Travel With

Our groups are made up of solo travellers, close friends, couples, and anyone who believes the best journeys are the ones shared with like-minded people. We deliberately keep our groups small because we believe travel feels better with personal attention, genuine safety, and planning that takes the stress off your shoulders—so all you have to do is enjoy the experience.

"Many of our travellers started out as strangers. They returned home as lifelong friends."
